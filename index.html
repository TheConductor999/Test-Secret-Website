Genesis Think Tank

Transforming Northern New England’s mental health ecosystem through systems thinking and Baldrige excellence.

A Coalition of Leaders for Systemic Change

The Genesis Think Tank unites CEOs and civic leaders who are committed to reshaping the macro ecosystem of Northern New England. Guided by the Baldrige Framework, the collective applies systems thinking to spark collaborative, measurable change.

Tom Raffio, CEO & President of Northeast Delta Dental and 2024 Malcolm Baldrige National Quality Award recipient, energizes the movement as its champion.
Brian Hettrick, Executive Director of ExcellenceNorth Alliance, serves as Chief Guide and systems strategist for the coalition.

Our Mission: A Systems Approach to Youth Mental Health

Genesis is mapping the mental health ecosystem of Northern New England with a special emphasis on youth. By understanding drivers, feedback loops, and interdependencies, the Think Tank is aligning policy, resources, and community action.

  • Identify system dynamics that influence access, equity, and long-term outcomes.
  • Shift stakeholders from siloed interventions to coordinated, systemic solutions.
  • Address root causes to build a resilient future for young people and families.
